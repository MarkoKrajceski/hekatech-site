Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's exactly how we handle your data: we don't.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how HekaTech ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile application Open Packs, Collect Cards (package name: com.hekatech.openpacks), available on the Google Play Store.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (none), how we use it (not applicable), and your rights.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data whatsoever.

  • No personal information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • No device identifiers or hardware information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies or tracking technologies

The app functions entirely offline. No user accounts or login are required.

Local Storage

The app uses on-device storage (SharedPreferences / local storage) solely to save your card collection progress. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Is never accessed by us
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

How We Use Your Data

Since we collect no data, there is no data to use. We do not process, analyze, or monetize any user information.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The app contains:

  • No advertisements
  • No analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • No third-party SDKs that collect user data
  • No social media integrations that track users

Data Transmission

No data is transmitted anywhere. The app does not make any network requests. It functions entirely offline with no internet permission required.

Children's Privacy

Open Packs, Collect Cards is rated PG-12 and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. However, since the app collects no personal data of any kind, it is inherently compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar international regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
